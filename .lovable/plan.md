

## Kult Technology — Minimalistic Futuristic Landing Page

A clean, light-themed showcase page that puts your brand front and center with elegant simplicity and modern polish.

---

### 🎨 Visual Design

**Color Palette:**
- Pure white background with light gray accents
- Black typography matching your logo's aesthetic
- Subtle shadows and clean lines for depth

**Typography:**
- Modern sans-serif font
- Bold weight for "kult" to match logo style
- Light/regular weight for supporting text

---

### 📄 Page Structure

**Hero Section (Full Viewport)**
- Your Kult Technology logo prominently centered
- A short, impactful tagline beneath (e.g., "Shaping the future of technology")
- Subtle fade-in animation on page load

**Brief Introduction**
- 1-2 sentences about what Kult Technology does
- Clean, minimal typography with generous whitespace

**Contact Section**
- Simple email link with hover effect
- Minimal footer with copyright

---

### ✨ Animations & Interactions

- Smooth fade-in animations as elements enter view
- Elegant hover effects on the contact email link
- Subtle scale transitions on interactive elements
- Optional: gentle floating/parallax effect on decorative elements

---

### 📱 Responsive Design

- Perfectly centered layout that works on all screen sizes
- Touch-friendly contact link for mobile visitors
- Consistent spacing and proportions across devices
